Design (with more details)

Niffler is a cloud-based vendor-free framework (to switch to any vendor you want) for trading using the tick-level data feed , we process and store over 100GB of data per day.

It could provide signal breakthrough notification, live curve, volatility surface calibration, and risk calculation

The researcher/trader could leverage its ample market data to rebuild the market structure and back-test their trading strategies with the highest accuracy

(Currently, the Niffler prod repo is private, it is the demo repo, and the application will be available soon)
